A little about my church

I am a member of the Church of God Reformation Movement of Barbados, more specifically the Church of God at Chelsea Road. We are a Group of Evangelical churches and our mission at the Church of God at Chelsea Road through the power of the Holy Spirit is to, connect people to Jesus through dynamic ministry of the Word, energetic worship, unconditional love and effective outreach programs, while cultivating the gifts and talents of the Body of Christ. At my church I serve as the co-secretary and a worship leader.
